about me

education

I completed a BSc (Psychology) with Honours at the Australian National University (ANU) between 2008-2011. I then held a research assistant position for one year, before begining PhD at ANU in 2013. During my PhD I combined psychophysical and computational techniques to study the limits of parallel motion processing and the constraints of short-term memory consolidation, under the supervision of A. Prof Mark Edwards. I completed my PhD in 2016.
